#Online Art Gallery
This is a simple online art gallery that showcases different paintings and their history.

#Structure
The website is all mostly static primarily to imitate the feel of a Online Art Gallery, therefore there isn't any functions for the button you will encounter.

#Navigation
Easily navigate the website just by going up and down and clicking the buttons to further learn about the paintings that have been featured.

#Paintings
Most of the paintings you will see in the website are taken from the link posted below.
#link
https://www.nga.gov/collection-search-result.html?sortOrder=DEFAULT&artobj_downloadable=Image_download_available&pageSize=30&pageNumber=5&lastFacet=artobj_downloadable
